Legacy
- Nkrumah's legacy continues to inspire Africans and people of African descent around the world.
- He is remembered as a champion of African liberation and unity.
- His ideas about African socialism and unity continue to influence contemporary debates about Africa's development.
- He is also remembered for his strong leadership and vision for Ghana and Africa.
